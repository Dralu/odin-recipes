Description

This is the typical dessert from Brittany

Ingredients

  • 250g flour
  • 125g sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 500ml whole milk

Steps

  1. Pour the flour and sugar inside a bowl
  2. Break the 3 eggs in and mix it all
  3. Pour the milk in three thirds while mixing it together
  4. cook it for 15 seconds on each side on a proper crepe cooking pan
  5. Serve it with homemade caramel
